Lasagna

Description

A delicious lasagna with a cheese filling and a hearty, handmade tomato sauce. The lasagna is prepared with a blend of cottage cheese and Parmesan, rather than ricotta as is traditionally done. Recommended side dishes are a crisp garlic bread and a fresh green salad.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
