Corn Dogs

Description

An easy-to-make corn dog that you make at home. Similar to the street food or carnival snack you can buy, the sausage is skewered and coated with cornmeal batter and best served with ketchup or masturd.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal and flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Frankfurters

Steps

  1. Make the batter by combining the dry ingredients, then stirring in the milk and egg.
  2. Skewer the frankfurters and roll in the batter.
  3. Fry the corn dogs in hot oil until lightly browned.
